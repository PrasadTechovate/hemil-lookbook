KNOW ME, A LITTLE

How I see · How I decide · How I collaborate

I look for where the emotional weight is sitting — who holds power and who is exposed. That tells me where the camera should stand.
The camera moves only when the character’s perspective shifts. If nothing changes internally, the camera stays quiet.
I reduce the scene to one believable source and shape around it. Less fixtures, more control.
I think about VFX at script stage — clean plates, locked exposure, consistent direction. Assumptions create problems later.
Technical continuity and visual coherence. That responsibility is mine.
